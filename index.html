About Me

I am driven by understanding the world around me. My attention has been primarily focused on understanding the brain throughout most of my higher education. It is here that my passion for problem solving grew through the implementation of experimental design. Being surrounded by technology and an introductory to MatLab, turned me on to the world of programming. During my graduate program in neuroscience I began to teach myself programming basics on the side through online resources, Codecademy and Udemy primarily. In doing so I discovered another passion, development. I was able to explore a creative side I didn't know existed while still being able to exercise my need for logical problem solving.

I am a man of many interests, long time gamer, once upon a time a college soccer player, avid movie goer and philosophical enthusiast. If creativity and problem solving are involved I'm in. This is to say that I enjoy all things development. That said, it is a goal of mine to, at some point in my career, bring my two passions together. Neuroscience and development. Whatever form that my come in I will strive to make it a reality.
